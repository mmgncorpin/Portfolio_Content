About Me

Quite the contradiction of myself, I am an introvert-leaning ambivert. I like to travel and see the world, but can go for days without going out of my house. I like being alone, and being with people I love.

I'm self-conscious of my body, but I love to cook and eat, and abhor exercise. And speaking of cooking, I'd like to think I'm pretty good at it. I haven't heard complaints yet!

Career-wise, I've gone through weird paths to get here. Straight out of College, I went into Customer Service, did a short stint in Advertising and Marketing, went back to school to fulfill my mother's dream for me to be a Registered Nurse, got a job as a Company Nurse/Human Resources Associate, became an HR Manager, an Implementations Specialist for an HR-Payroll Software Company, and finally, a Medication Care Manager. Whew!

I used to blog here, but completely abandoned it in 2016. Due to boredome, I guess.

Why the Career Change?

  • I finally realized that Nursing is NOT for me;
  • I've worked with Front-End & Back-End Developers, and I thought what they did was so cool!;
  • I wanted to learn how to create these sites, viewed across the world;
  • I want to find the career I can work with while traveling (or staying at home!);
  • I get instant gratification in seeing the output of my "creation."
